Core Areas of Expertise

A blend of scientific precision and analytical tools, turning raw data into actionable insights.

Collect

Drawing on my scientific background, I methodically source data from spreadsheets, SQL databases, public datasets, and web platforms. I focus on gathering the right data with the structure and reliability needed for serious analysis.

Process

Using Excel and SQL, I transform raw inputs into clean, structured datasets ready for analysis. My background in the lab sharpened my attention to detail, a skill I apply rigorously when filtering data and preparing consistent information for meaningful insights.

Visualize

I design dashboards and visuals with Tableau and Power BI that speak clearly to decision-makers. My goal is always the same: extract meaning, highlight trends, and present complex results in a way that makes sense. Even to a non-technical audience.
